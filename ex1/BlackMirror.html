Blackmirror

Black Mirror is a British science fiction anthology television series created by Charlie Brooker.
It examines modern society, particularly with regard to the unanticipated consequences of new technologies.

Best Quotes from the series

  1. So many choices, you end up not knowing which one you want.
  2. You are a top bird. I don’t know what that means, but I heard someone say it once and you’re it.
